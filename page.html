( About me )

I build digital products with a focus on craftsmanship. My background in UX/product design and front-end development allows me to carry ideas from early concept to fully implemented systems without losing their intent. I value clean structure, consistency, and code that supports the design system.


I use storytelling, animation, and character to give each project a distinct voice and sense of life. Outside of work, I spend time on personal passion projects and small games with friends, combining art, writing, and code to explore new ideas. My work is about creating interfaces that feel considered, reliable, and well made.
